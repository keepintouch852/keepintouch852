Keep in Touch — July Gatherings Keep in Touch — 七月聚會

This July, Keep in Touch opens its doors to a series of intimate gatherings shaped by food, tea, sound, touch and story.

Some unfold around a table. Some bring us closer, gathered in a circle. Some begin with a meal, a cup of tea, a sound — and become something we could never quite plan.

ichigo ichie One time, one meeting.

No gathering can ever be repeated in quite the same way.

Who we find ourselves beside, what is shared around the table, the conversations that unfold, the unexpected moments in between — all of it belongs only to that time, that place, that meeting.

Perhaps that is the beauty of gathering.

We come together for a little while. We share what is here. And we let each encounter become what it wants to become.

這個七月，Keep in Touch 將打開空間，邀請你走進一系列由食物、茶、聲音、觸感與故事交織而成的小型聚會。

有些，在餐桌上慢慢展開。有些，讓我們圍坐在一起，靠近彼此。有些，從一頓飯、一杯茶、一段聲音開始——然後，走向我們未曾預想的地方。

一期一會 一時一遇

沒有一次相聚，能夠以完全相同的方式再次發生。

那天與誰坐在彼此身旁、餐桌上分享了什麼、自然展開的對話，還有那些意料之外的片刻——一切，都只屬於那個時間、那個地方、那一次相遇。

也許，這就是相聚的美好。

我們短暫相聚，分享此刻所有，也讓每一次相遇，自然成為它想成為的模樣。

An afternoon of nourishment, deep listening, tea and sound.

What might we hear when we give ourselves the time to truly listen?

We begin around the table with a seasonal, nourishing lunch prepared by Carina — a meal to be enjoyed slowly, with attention to what is before us and the people beside us.

From there, the afternoon opens into a shared practice of listening — to one another, to the spaces between words, and to what is quietly moving within us.

With tea by @aourae.tea and sound by @kawainflow, we journey further inward, allowing taste, vibration and silence to guide us through our inner landscapes.

There is nowhere we need to arrive. Only an afternoon to nourish, to listen, to feel — and to see what unfolds.

一個關於滋養、聆聽、茶與聲音的午後。

當我們真正留一點時間，好好聆聽——會聽見什麼？

我們從餐桌開始。由 Carina 準備當季滋養午餐，慢慢品嚐眼前的食物，也感受此刻與我們同坐的人。

飯後，我們把步調再放慢一點，讓聆聽自然發生——聽見彼此，聽見言語之間的留白，也聽見內在那些正在悄悄流動的感受。

隨著 @aourae.tea 的茶與 @kawainflow 的聲音，我們再往內走一點。讓味道、振動與寂靜，引領我們流入各自的內在風景。

我們不需要抵達哪裡。只需要一個午後，好好滋養，好好聆聽，好好感受——讓這個午後，自然帶我們去它想去的地方。

An intimate journey through tea, vibration and taste.

When the sound fades, what remains?

We begin with tea. A quiet ceremony guided by @aourae.tea — a moment to arrive, to settle, and to notice what is already here.

From there, we move into the deep resonance of gong with @mettawellnesshk — allowing waves of sound and vibration to travel through the body, carrying us beyond the need for words.

And when the sound comes to rest, we return slowly to the senses. Tea is poured again, this time accompanied by a thoughtful pairing of food prepared by @carina_heting. After travelling through sound, we return to taste slowly — noticing flavour, texture, temperature, and the quiet pleasure of sharing food together.

An afternoon that begins with tea, travels through sound, and returns us to taste.

一場穿梭於茶、振動與味覺之間的午後。

當聲音漸漸退去，留下來的是什麼？

我們從一杯茶開始。由 @aourae.tea 引領一席安靜的茶席，讓自己慢慢抵達，安頓下來，也留意此刻已然存在的一切。

然後，我們隨 @mettawellnesshk 走進銅鑼深沉而綿延的共振之中。讓一層又一層的聲音與振動穿過身體，在無需言語的片刻裡，只是聆聽，只是感受。

當最後的餘韻慢慢落下，我們再次回到眼前的感官世界。茶再度注入杯中，伴隨由 @carina_heting 準備的精心食物配搭。經歷聲音的流動之後，我們慢慢回到味覺——留意風味、質感、溫度，也重新感受一起分享食物，那份安靜而簡單的愉悅。

一個從茶開始，穿過聲音，最後回到味覺的午後。

Some places stay with us long after we leave.

In a taste. A scent. A conversation with someone we may never meet again. A way of seeing, living, or being that quietly follows us home.

Over the past few years, we have returned to Bali again and again. Somewhere along the way, it became more than a place we visited. Through the land, the food, the people we met and the warmth with which we were welcomed, Bali found a special place in our hearts.

This July, we invite eight guests around the Keep in Touch table for two intimate evenings of food, drinks and stories.

Not to recreate Bali. But to share something of our relationship with it — the places that moved us, the people who welcomed us with such warmth, the flavours we keep thinking about, and the moments we are still carrying home.

Throughout the evening, a vegetarian menu unfolds alongside thoughtful drink pairings and stories gathered along the way.

Some will be told. Some will be tasted. Some may simply be felt.

A meal shaped by memory, curiosity and gratitude.

For two evenings only, come makan with us.

有些地方，離開以後，依然會留在我們心裡很久。

可能是一種味道。一縷氣息。一場與某個也許不會再見的人之間的對話。又或者，是某種看待世界、生活與存在的方式，悄悄跟著我們回了家。

過去幾年，我們一次又一次回到峇里島。不知從什麼時候開始，它已不再只是一個我們到訪的地方。那片土地、那裡的食物、一路遇見的人，還有一次又一次被溫暖接住的感覺，讓峇里島慢慢在我們心裡，成為了一個很特別的存在。

這個七月，我們邀請八位客人來到 Keep in Touch 的餐桌旁，一起度過兩個關於食物、飲品與故事的晚上。

不是為了重現峇里島。而是想與你分享，我們與這個地方之間的一點故事——那些曾經觸動我們的地方、以溫暖迎接我們的人、至今仍然念念不忘的味道，還有一些，我們依然帶在身上、帶回生活裡的片刻。

整個晚上，純素菜單將隨著精心配搭的飲品與沿途收集的故事，慢慢展開。

有些，化成故事。有些，融進味道。有些，也許只留在感受裡。

一頓由記憶、好奇與感恩慢慢醞釀而成的晚餐。

只此兩夜，來和我們一起 makan，一起吃飯吧。

This afternoon begins with a hand-carved rose pearl.

Formed slowly over time, each natural pearl carries its own unique lustre. In the hands of an artisan, it is carefully carved into a rose in bloom — no two ever quite the same, each carrying traces of time within it.

Together, we will weave a pearl bracelet of our own. As thread moves between our fingers, we allow the hands to find their rhythm and the mind to slowly settle into the making. Knot by knot, something begins to take form — a piece that is entirely your own.

After weaving, we gather around small bites and tea prepared by @carina_heting, sharing whatever feelings or stories are present in that moment.

Here, you are simply you. There is nothing to achieve this afternoon, and no one you need to become.

Only the lustre of pearls, the feeling of creating with our hands, the taste of food and tea, and the moments we find in meeting one another.

Together, we weave. We taste. We stay with the moment, and quietly feel.

Includes: one hand-carved rose pearl, 925 sterling silver components, all weaving materials, and small bites and tea prepared by @carina_heting.

這個下午，我們將以一顆手工雕刻的玫瑰珍珠作為開始。

天然珍珠經過歲月孕育，形成獨特的光澤，再由工匠以雙手細細雕刻成一朵盛放的玫瑰。每一顆都獨一無二，也承載著時間留下的痕跡。

在聚會裡，我們會親手編織一條屬於自己的珍珠手繩。讓雙手慢慢跟上呼吸，讓思緒在編織之間漸漸沉澱。線繩穿過指縫，一結一結，慢慢織出只屬於自己的模樣。

編織過後，我們會一起品嚐由 @carina_heting 親手製作的精緻茶點與茶飲，圍坐在一起，分享當下的感受與故事。

暫時放下日常的繁雜，也放下我們習慣扮演的角色。此刻，你只是你。

這個下午，沒有需要完成的目標，也不需要成為任何人。只有珍珠的光、雙手的觸感、食物與茶的滋味，還有彼此相遇的片刻。

一起織光，一起品味，一起在當下，靜靜感受。
